In Account Center, click the **Account Settings** tab to manage your personal information and login password.

<img src="/assets/images/en/admin/account-center-settings_en.webp" alt="Account settings" />

## Personal Information

Basic account info is shown read-only:

| Field | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Username** | The username you registered with. |
| **Email** | The email address linked to your account. |

<Callout type="info">
To change your username or email, contact your organization administrator or submit a support ticket.
</Callout>

## Change Password

Follow these steps to change your login password:

1. Enter your old password in the **Current Password** field to verify your identity.
2. Set a new password in the **New Password** field (at least 6 characters).
3. Re-enter the same new password in the **Confirm New Password** field.
4. Click **Save**.

After a successful change, use the new password the next time you sign in.

<Callout type="warn">
For account security, change your password periodically and avoid reusing the same password across other platforms.
</Callout>
