## Ticket Submission Flow

Click **Submit Ticket** to open the enterprise ticketing system:

1. **Authentication:** Sign in (WeChat QR login supported).
2. **Fill the form:** Describe the issue in detail.
3. **Upload evidence:** Attach screenshots from **Application Information** and **Docker error logs**.

## Service Response SLA

Official response times:

* **Business hours:** Mon-Fri 10:00 - 18:00 (GMT+8).
* **General issues:** Response within **7 business days** (feature questions, non-blocking bugs).
* **Urgent issues:** Response within **24 hours**.

<Callout type="error">
**Definition of urgent issues:** Only when the system is **completely unavailable** (cannot start, no one can log in), **core data loss**, or **major security vulnerabilities**.

**First response time** means the time when support accepts and replies to the ticket, not the resolution time. We will make reasonable efforts, but do not guarantee exact fix timing.
</Callout>
