### Modify Configuration

Click **Settings** in the knowledge base list to edit name, description, and rerank model strategy.

<img src="/assets/images/en/admin/knowledge-base-maintenance-tuning_en.webp" alt="" />
<Callout type="info">
**When to change chunk size?** If AI responses often quote partial content with missing context, the **chunk size** may be too small. If responses include lots of irrelevant text, the **chunk size** may be too large. Changing this usually requires re-uploading/processing documents.
</Callout>

### Validate Retrieval Quality

To ensure the knowledge base is configured correctly:

1. Go to **Assistant Management** and create a test assistant.
2. In **Knowledge Base Settings**, associate the newly created knowledge base.
3. In the preview panel or client, ask a question that exists only in this knowledge base (e.g., "What is our late-arrival deduction policy?").
4. **Success indicator:** The assistant answers correctly and hovering over the answer shows a **citation** pointing to your document.
