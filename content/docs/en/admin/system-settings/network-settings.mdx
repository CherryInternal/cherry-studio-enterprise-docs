## Configuration Steps

1. Go to **System Settings** > **Network Settings**.
2. Ensure your enterprise proxy server/gateway is running properly (e.g., when the intranet must reach external APIs through a unified egress).
3. Enter the proxy address below.

## Proxy Port Reference

Listening ports vary by proxy software/gateway. Use the "local listening port" shown in your actual proxy service settings, and fill in the corresponding `http://<address>:<port>`.

<Callout type="warn">
**Note**:

1. Do not use `socks5://` — Cherry Studio currently supports mainly `http://` proxies.
2. After configuration, go to **Model Management** and click **Check** to verify connectivity.
</Callout>
