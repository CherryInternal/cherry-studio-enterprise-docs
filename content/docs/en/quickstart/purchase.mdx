# Purchase and License

## Version Selection

Cherry Studio Enterprise offers multiple editions. Choose based on team size and needs:

| Edition | Use Case | User Count |
|------|----------|--------|
| Standard | Small teams | Up to 50 users |
| Professional | Mid-sized enterprises | Up to 200 users |
| Enterprise | Large organizations | Unlimited |

## Obtain a License

1. Visit the [Cherry Studio website](https://cherry-ai.com) to purchase
2. After payment, you will receive a license email
3. The email contains your license key and download link

## Next Step

After obtaining a license, continue to [Deploy the service](/en/quickstart/deploy).
