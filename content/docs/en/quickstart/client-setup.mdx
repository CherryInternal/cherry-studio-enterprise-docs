# Client Setup

The Cherry Studio client is the AI assistant app used by employees daily.

## Download the Client

Download the client for your operating system from the official website:

- **macOS**: [Download DMG](https://cherry-ai.com/download/mac)
- **Windows**: [Download EXE](https://cherry-ai.com/download/windows)
- **Linux**: [Download AppImage](https://cherry-ai.com/download/linux)

## Configure the Enterprise Server

When you open the client for the first time, configure the connection to your enterprise server:

1. Open the client and go to **Settings**
2. Find the **Enterprise Server** configuration
3. Enter the server address: `http://your-server:3670`
4. Click **Connect**

## Sign In

Sign in with the account created by the admin:

1. Enter username and password
2. Click **Sign In**
3. After signing in, you will see the available AI models and assistants
